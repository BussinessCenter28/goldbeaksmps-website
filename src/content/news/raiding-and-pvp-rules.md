GoldBeak is a **raiding-allowed** server, and PvP is part of the core experience.
Here's how it works so there are no surprises.

## Raiding

Bases are fair game. There is **no grief protection** out in the world, so:

- Hide your valuables and build real defenses.
- Expect to be raided — and plan your revenge.
- Outsmarting raiders (and raiders outsmarting you) is half the fun.

## PvP

- PvP is **enabled** everywhere except **spawn**, which is a protected safe zone.
- You **cannot** run into spawn while in combat to escape a fight. Doing so breaks the rules.
- No **naked killing** — don't farm unarmed players.

## Always banned

Hacking, X-ray, duping, and autoclickers will get you removed. Play fair, fight
hard, and may the best builder win.

Check the full [rules](/#rules) any time.
