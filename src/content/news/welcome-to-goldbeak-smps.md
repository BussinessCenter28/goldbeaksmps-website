Welcome to **GoldBeak SMPS** — a crossplay survival server for Java and Bedrock
players alike. Whether you're here to build a fortress, raid a rival, or grind
your way up the economy, there's a place for you in our world.

## What makes GoldBeak different

- **Crossplay.** Java and Bedrock players share one world. Bring your friends, no matter their device.
- **Real survival.** No hand-holding and no pay-to-win. Everything you have, you earned.
- **PvP & raiding.** Combat is on out in the world, and bases are fair game. Spawn stays a safe zone.
- **A player economy.** Build a balance, trade, and climb the ranks.

## Getting started

1. Copy the server address from the [home page](../../#join).
2. Add it in Minecraft (Java or Bedrock).
3. Jump in, read the rules, and start your story.

See you in-game. Don't get raided. 🔥
